# About Me

At my day job I'm paid to write code, but I might write full sentences on this site.

I use Rust and I maintain some open source projects. I'm slowly getting into NixOS while writing
this so maybe that's what I'll also write about here, maybe I just abandon it in the next few weeks.
Maybe we'll find out together.

## This Site

I don't know what's the raison d'être for this site yet. I _think_ I write it mostly for myself to
partly document what I've been doing, partly to have something to go back to (and maybe laugh at)
some day, partly to just see if I can do this and not be embarrassed with the result.

But another part also is to provide information on topics some other people might find useful.
